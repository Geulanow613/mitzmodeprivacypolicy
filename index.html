Mitz Mode© Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 19, 2025

Introduction

Mitz Mode ("we", "our", or "app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our app handles permissions and data.

Permissions Used

Internet Access

  • Used for sending mitzvah submissions via email
  • Used for error tracking via Sentry
  • Used to fetch updated mitzvot content from our GitHub repository

Data Collection and Storage

Local Storage

  • Completed mitzvot are stored locally
  • No user data is synchronized with external servers
  • All data is removed when the app is uninstalled
  • The app automatically resets checklist items daily at midnight
  • Previous day's data is permanently deleted from local storage during reset

Cloud Content

  • The app fetches additional mitzvot content from our GitHub repository
  • This content is public and contains no personal information
  • No user data is sent to GitHub when fetching content
  • GitHub's privacy policy applies to content delivery (available at GitHub Privacy Statement)
  • Content updates do not affect your personal progress or completed mitzvot

Personal Information

  • We do not collect any personal information beyond voluntary mitzvah submissions
  • We do not track location
  • We do not access contacts
  • We do not collect device information except for error tracking
  • We do not use analytics services

Mitzvah Submission Feature

Android Version

  • Users can submit mitzvah suggestions via an in-app form
  • The following information is collected:
    • User's name (as provided)
    • Mitzvah description text
  • This information is transmitted via email to app moderators
  • No information is stored locally after submission

iOS Version

  • Users can submit mitzvah suggestions via an in-app form
  • The following information is collected:
    • User's name (as provided)
    • Mitzvah description text
  • Submissions are sent to a private GitHub repository accessible only to app moderators
  • GitHub's privacy policy applies to submissions (available at GitHub Privacy Statement)
  • No information is stored locally after submission

Data Transmission

  • Android: Submissions are sent via secure email (SMTP)
  • iOS: Submissions are sent via secure HTTPS to a private GitHub repository
  • Data is transmitted only to app moderators
  • Submissions are used only for app content moderation
  • No data is shared with third parties beyond the submission process

Data Retention and Display

  • Submissions are retained for moderation purposes
  • Submissions may be edited or rejected
  • Accepted submissions become part of the app's mitzvot database
  • Submitter's name may be included alongside their accepted mitzvah in the app
  • By submitting a mitzvah, users consent to having their provided name displayed within the app

Features

Video Playback

  • The app contains links to external video content (e.g., TorahAnytime.com)
  • These videos are hosted on third-party websites
  • When accessing external video content:
    • You will be directed to the external website
    • The third-party website's privacy policy and terms of service will apply
    • We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these external sites
  • No video usage tracking is done by our app

External Links

  • Our app may contain links to external websites
  • When you click these links, you will be directed to third-party websites which have their own privacy policies and terms of service
  • We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of any external websites
  • We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any external sites you visit through our app

Error Tracking

  • We use Sentry to collect error reports when the app experiences technical issues
  • Error reports include:
    • Device model and OS version
    • Time and date of error
    • Technical details about the error
    • Which screen or feature was being used
    • Basic network information (connection type)
    • App version
  • This data helps us identify and fix problems to improve app stability
  • No personal information is included in error reports
  • Error data is automatically deleted after 90 days
  • IP addresses are anonymized

Third-Party Services

We use Sentry (sentry.io), an error tracking service, to help improve app stability. Sentry's privacy policy can be found at https://sentry.io/privacy/. No other third-party services or analytics platforms are used.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. While we do not collect personal information beyond voluntary mitzvah submissions, parents or guardians should supervise children's use of the submission feature.

Data Deletion

  • All app data is stored locally on your device
  • Uninstalling the app removes all associated data
  • Submitted mitzvot and names remain in the app's content if accepted
  • Contact us to request removal of your submitted content
  • Error tracking data is automatically deleted after 90 days

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
