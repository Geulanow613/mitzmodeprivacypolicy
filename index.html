Introduction

Mitz Mode ("we", "our", or "app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our app handles permissions and data.

Permissions Used

Internet Access

  • Used for sending mitzvah submissions
  • Used for error tracking via Sentry
  • Used to fetch mitzvot content from our public repository

Data Collection and Storage

Local Storage

  • Completed mitzvot are stored locally on your device
  • No user data is synchronized with external servers
  • All data is removed when the app is uninstalled
  • The app automatically resets checklist items daily at midnight

Mitzvah Submission Feature

Android Version

  • Users can submit mitzvah suggestions via an in-app form
  • The following information is collected:
    • User's name (as provided)
    • Mitzvah description text
  • This information is transmitted via email to app moderators
  • No information is stored locally after submission

iOS Version

  • Users can submit mitzvah suggestions via Formspree
  • The following information is collected:
    • User's name (as provided)
    • Mitzvah description text
  • Submissions are processed through Formspree's secure service
  • No information is stored locally after submission

Error Tracking

  • We use Sentry to collect error reports when the app experiences technical issues
  • Error reports include:
    • Device model and OS version
    • Time and date of error
    • Technical details about the error
    • App version
  • This data helps us identify and fix problems
  • No personal information is included in error reports
  • Error data is automatically deleted after 90 days

Third-Party Services

  • Sentry (sentry.io) for error tracking - Privacy Policy
  • Formspree for iOS form submissions - Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Deletion

  • All app data is stored locally on your device
  • Uninstalling the app removes all associated data
  • Contact us to request removal of your submitted content

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
